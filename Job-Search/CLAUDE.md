## Key Rules

- CV format: .docx for ATS submissions
- Achievement formula: [Strategic Action Verb] + [Enterprise Initiative] + [Strategic Approach] + [Quantified Business Impact]
- Use enterprise-scale metrics ($M/$B, not $K); reference EBITDA, margins, enterprise value
- Avoid: "Responsible for", "Helped", "Assisted" — use strategic authority verbs
- Target 2-3 pages; focus on last 15-20 years
- Tailor executive summary to each specific opportunity
