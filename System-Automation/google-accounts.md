### 1. Personal account vs. Workspace account

Should the primary "power" account be a personal Google account or the Workspace account? Key considerations:

- Personal AI Pro is slightly cheaper for equivalent AI features and includes the $10/month developer API credit (not available on Workspace's Gemini add-on)
- Personal AI Pro includes 5 TB storage vs. 2 TB on Workspace Business Pro (even with the Gemini add-on)
- Workspace provides a custom domain email (usman@experimentoflife.org), which looks more professional for EoL

## Plan Comparison

### Personal Google Plans

| Plan | Price | Storage | AI / Gemini | Other benefits |
|------|-------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Google One Premium | €10/month | 2 TB | Some AI features, lower tier | Longer Google Meet calls |
| Google AI Pro | €20/month | 5 TB | Full AI usage, higher tier | $10/month developer API credit for Google AI |

### Google Workspace Plans

| Plan | Price | Storage | AI / Gemini | Other benefits |
|------|-------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Business Pro (current) | ~€12/month | 2 TB | Better Gemini tier than personal base, but not equivalent to AI Pro | Meet recordings, Meet notes |
| Business Pro + Gemini add-on | ~€20–22/month | 2 TB | Roughly equivalent to Google AI Pro tier | As above — but **no $10 developer API credit** |

### Side-by-Side: Google AI Pro vs. Workspace Business Pro + Gemini Add-on

| Factor | Google AI Pro (personal) | Workspace Business Pro + Gemini add-on |
|--------|--------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Monthly cost | €20 | ~€20–22 |
| Storage | 5 TB | 2 TB |
| AI/Gemini level | Full | Roughly equivalent |
| Developer API credit | $10/month | No |
| Custom domain email | No | Yes (usman@experimentoflife.org) |
| Meet recordings & notes | Yes (with AI Pro) | Yes |
| Business admin controls | No | Yes |
| Verdict | Cheaper, more storage, dev credit | Professional email, business features |
