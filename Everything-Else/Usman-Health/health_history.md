## Notes for AI Analysis

### Inflammation Threshold Model (Established 2026-04-14)

The body has an inflammation "cup" that overflows at a threshold, triggering sickness behavior (malaise, internal heat, feeling sick). Sickness behavior is driven by cytokine levels in the brain (via vagus nerve), NOT by temperature — same 37.5°C can feel fine or terrible.

**Four hits filling the cup:**
1. **Obesity** (BMI 32.4) — fat tissue constantly produces low-level inflammatory cytokines. Permanent baseline.
2. **Chronic sinusitis / gut dysbiosis** — active immune response producing cytokines. Ongoing until resolved.
3. **Dietary triggers** — sugar/processed food disrupts gut barrier → bacterial fragments leak into blood → cytokine surge 12-18 hours later. Controllable.
4. **Mental stress** — activates NF-kB (same inflammatory switch as sugar), produces the same cytokines. Controllable via breathing/vagal tone.

Hits 1+2 keep near threshold. Hits 3 and/or 4 push over it.
