# Current Recommendations — Daily Action Plan

## #1: Diet (Zero Sugar While Infected)

What you eat tonight determines how you feel tomorrow (12-18 hour delay). Your inflammation cup is near the rim — sugar/processed food overflows it.

**Strict avoid:**
- Sugar (sweets, ice cream, sugary drinks)
- Non-fermented dairy (milk, cheese, cream sauces)
- Processed/fatty foods, spiced tea

**Safe to order:**
- Grilled chicken + white rice + steamed vegetables
- Plain grilled salmon/fish + rice (no heavy sauces)
- Chicken soup / broth-based soups (not creamy)
- Plain chicken shawarma plate with rice (no garlic sauce/dairy dips)
- Poke bowl: rice + salmon/chicken + avocado + cucumber (no spicy mayo)

**Safe drinks:**
- Black coffee (anti-inflammatory)
- Kefir (fermented = anti-inflammatory, rebuilds gut bacteria)
- Ginger tea
- Water — 3-4L daily

**Anti-inflammatory snacks:**
- Walnuts (handful daily, plain/raw)
- Blueberries

## #2: Gut Healing (Likely Primary Treatment)

The gut is probably the main fever driver now — not just supportive care.

**Phase 1 (Days 1-3):**
- L-glutamine powder — 5g on empty stomach, 2x daily (repairs gut barrier)
- Bone broth — 1-2 cups daily

**Phase 2 (Days 4-10) — add:**
- Megaspore Biotic probiotic — 1 capsule daily with breakfast
- Omega-3 (Nordic Naturals) — 1-2 tsp daily with food

## #3: Anti-Inflammatory Supplements

- Curcumin with piperine — 1 capsule daily (blocks NF-kB, the master inflammatory switch)
- Vitamin D — 2000-4000 IU daily
- Omega-3 (same as gut healing Phase 2)

## #4: Sinus Routine (Twice Daily)

1. Saline spray — clean the nose
2. Mometasone spray — after saline

## #5: Medications

- **Mounjaro** — keep taking; anti-inflammatory + appetite control
- **Lisdexamfetamine (Elvanse)** — maintain daily; prevents binge eating

## #6: Stress Management

Slow breathing (4s in, 6-8s out, 5-10 min) — not relaxation advice, it physically activates the vagus nerve which suppresses cytokine production. Do when stressed or feeling the sickness coming on.

## #7: Sleep

- Head elevation: 3-4 pillows
- Humidifier at night

## Quick Relief (When Feeling Sick)

- Ibuprofen 400mg — blocks PGE2, reduces sickness feeling within 30-45 min
- Large glass of cold water (500ml)
- Cool compress on forehead/neck
