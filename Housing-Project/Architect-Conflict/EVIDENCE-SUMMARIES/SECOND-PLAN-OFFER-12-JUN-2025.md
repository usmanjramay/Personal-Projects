## PROJECT OBJECTIVE

**Scope:**
> "Change of use of a single-family home into a 3-4 apartment building, as per the attached sketch, has been accepted by both parties. Various modifications may be necessary to comply with municipal regulations. Significant changes to the building's layout and organization cannot be made by the client once the preliminary design has been accepted; otherwise, additional charges will apply."

**Key Conditions:**
- The change into 3-4 apartments "has been accepted by both parties"
- Client cannot make significant changes after preliminary design without additional charges
- Project includes contingency for regulatory changes

---

## GENERAL INFORMATION ABOUT SERVICES

The architect will provide:
- Analysis of the municipal regulations and various other necessary regulations
- Site visit and measurements taken to develop an as-built plan
- Creation of an existing as-built plan of the existing house in digital format
- Feasibility studies
- Preliminary draft and proposed improvement
- Project design
- Calculation of surfaces for COS and CMU coefficients if necessary, and glazed surface area
- Application for building permit
- Plans for expansion and renovation for the building permit application:
  - Floor plan adapted for a residence and various modifications
  - Facades
  - Cut
  - Site plan
  - 2x Outdoor 3D Images
- Architect's Certificate OAI Order of Architects
- Printing of plans for the building permit application and for the client (2x municipality, 2x client)
- Request for advice from the CGDIS firefighters
- Request for advice from the various necessary departments
- Analysis of the existing building for renovation suitability
- Contacting the land registry administration for land measurement if necessary
- Request for advice from the CFL if necessary
- Application for building permit
- Sending the building permit application to the municipality
