## PROJECT OBJECTIVE

- The extension of the ground floor level

## KEY LEGAL POINTS FOR DISPUTES

1. **Third Payment is Conditional:** Third payment (25%) is **only due** upon obtaining the building permit — this is explicitly stated as a payment condition.

2. **Scope Limited to Contract:** The contract specifies that:
   - 3-apartment feasibility will be analyzed
   - 5-apartment analysis requires "a new offer to be issued"
   - Any work beyond the project program must be "offered separately"

3. **Client Obligation to Collaborate:** The contract states "The client agrees to continue collaborating with the same architectural firm for the various options analyzed by the architect" — but this is for analysis/discussion, not automatic payment for expanded scope.

4. **Additional Work Requires Separate Agreement:** The phrase "Additional requests from the administration or the client must be offered separately" is critical — it means new work requires separate pricing and agreement.
