## PROJECT OBJECTIVE

**Scope:**
> "The extension of the garden level and the extension of the ground floor level and the addition of a recessed floor.

> The plan will include the possibility and will be organized in such a way as to later create a vertical cadastral survey for a building with four officially registered apartments, each with an average surface area of approximately 80 square meters. The building will have a gross floor area of approximately 450 square meters. The actual area may vary depending on local regulations and applicable standards.

> The change of use of a single-family home into a four-unit apartment building, as per the attached sketch, has been accepted by both parties. Various modifications may be necessary to comply with municipal regulations. Significant changes to the building's layout and organization cannot be made by the client once the preliminary design has been accepted; otherwise, additional charges will apply."

**Highlighted in Yellow in Original:**
> "If during the development of the project the number of apartments is reduced for reasons of execution or regulation, a new joint agreement will have to be made in order to satisfy both parties."

This contingency clause is **critical evidence** because:
